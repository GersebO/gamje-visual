Why doesn't my fetch() request work? Unresolved

Asked by: Jessica Lee on June 14, 2025
I tried using fetch to get data from the API, but the console gives me a CORS error. Here's my code: 
fetch("http://localhost:8080/api")
  .then(res => res.json())
  .then(data => console.log(data));

What should I change?

Prof. Rojas: This error is due to CORS policy from the backend. You need to enable CORS headers in your Spring Boot controller or use a proxy for development.
Carlos Méndez: I had the same issue, I fixed it by adding @CrossOrigin to my controller class.
